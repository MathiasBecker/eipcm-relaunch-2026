Training

The European Institute for Participatory Media (EIPCM) offers specialized training programs designed to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI research and the practical, market-oriented needs of companies and public organizations. Our approach is trans-disciplinary, user-centered, and practice-driven, focusing on the responsible and effective use of artificial intelligence.

Our Training Pillars

01

Towards Reflective AI

We believe that harnessing the benefits of AI while preventing harm cannot be solved through regulation and technological fixes alone. Our training enables participants to understand AI and the consequences of its use and design, ensuring a foundation for responsible implementation.

02

Human-AI Interaction & User Experience (UX)

This module focuses on the design of intuitive and user-friendly interfaces. We train professionals to build systems that empower people to interact with AI effectively and confidently, prioritizing the human element in the technological loop.

03

AI Literacy & Combating Disinformation

In an era of shifting information landscapes, we provide tools for fostering critical thinking and media competence. Participants learn to understand AI's role in shaping information and develop strategies to combat online disinformation.

04

Sustainability & Science Communication

We offer insights into the societal impact of AI, promoting sustainable practices and effective communication strategies to address global challenges. This training helps organizations foster a better public understanding of how AI influences society at large.

Why EIPCM?

As a non-profit innovation facilitator, EIPCM leverages years of experience from international research projects (such as the Reflective AI project) and peer-reviewed scientific contributions. We don't just teach theory; we translate scientific inquiry into real-world applications for a better society.
